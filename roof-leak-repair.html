A leaking roof can cause significant damage to your property if left untreated. Our professional roof leak repair service in West Sussex provides fast, effective solutions to identify and fix leaks before they become major problems.

Common Causes of Roof Leaks

🧱 Damaged Tiles

Broken, cracked, or missing tiles allow water to penetrate your roof structure.

🔧 Failed Flashing

Flashing around chimneys, vents, and skylights can deteriorate over time, causing leaks.

🌊 Gutter Issues

Blocked or damaged gutters can cause water to back up and seep into the roof.

⏰ Age & Wear

Older roofs naturally deteriorate, with seals and materials breaking down over years.

Signs You Have a Roof Leak

💦 Water Stains

Discoloured patches on ceilings or walls indicate water ingress.

💧 Dripping Water

Active drips or water droplets in your attic or living spaces.

🦠 Mould Growth

Mould or mildew on walls, ceilings, or in the attic.

📉 Sagging Ceiling

Ceilings showing signs of water damage or sagging.
