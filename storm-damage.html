Professional Storm Damage Repairs

Severe weather can cause significant damage to your roof. Whether it's high winds, heavy rain, or hail, our storm damage repair service in West Sussex provides fast, professional repairs to restore your roof and protect your property.

Types of Storm Damage We Repair

💨 Wind Damage

Strong winds can lift tiles, damage flashing, and even cause structural damage to your roof.

🌨️ Hail Damage

Hail can dent, crack, or completely destroy roofing tiles, leading to leaks.

🌊 Flooding

Heavy rainfall can overwhelm gutters and cause water damage to your roof structure.

🌳 Fallen Debris

Storms can cause trees and branches to fall on your roof, causing major damage.

What to Do After Storm Damage

1. Ensure Safety

Stay indoors and avoid going into areas where debris may fall.

2. Document Damage

Take photographs of all visible damage for insurance purposes.

3. Contact Us

Call us for a professional assessment and quote.

4. Contact Insurer

Notify your insurance company and provide documentation.
