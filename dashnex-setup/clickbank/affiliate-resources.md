## HOW TO ACCESS THESE RESOURCES

### Step 1: Get Approved as Affiliate
1. Go to ClickBank Marketplace
2. Search for the product
3. Click "Promote" 
4. Fill out application (some products require approval)

### Step 2: Access Vendor Affiliate Dashboard
Once approved, you'll get access to:
- Your unique hoplinks
- Banner ads
- Email swipe files
- Free giveaway downloads
- Tracking statistics

### Step 3: Download & Customize
- Download email swipes
- Customize with your affiliate link
- Upload free reports to your site
- Add banners to landing pages

## RECOMMENDED WORKFLOW

1. **Apply to become affiliate** on ClickBank for your chosen product
2. **Log into vendor affiliate dashboard** 
3. **Download free reports/giveaways** - these become your lead magnets
4. **Get email swipes** - customize with your links
5. **Upload lead magnets** to your Dashnex site
6. **Set up email follow-up** in GetResponse (or your autoresponder)
7. **Drive traffic** to your landing pages
