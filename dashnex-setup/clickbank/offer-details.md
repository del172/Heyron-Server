## HOW TO GENERATE YOUR AFFILIATE LINKS

### Step 1: Log into ClickBank
https://accounts.clickbank.com

### Step 2: Go to Marketplace
https://www.clickbank.com/marketplace

### Step 3: Search for product
- Type product name in search
- Click on the product

### Step 4: Click "Promote" button
- Enter your account nickname
- Add tracking ID (e.g., "niche1", "weightloss", "pets")
- Click "Generate Hoplink"

### Your link will look like:
```
https://PRODUCTNAME.clickbank.jp/?affiliate=YOUR_NICKNAME&tid=YOUR_TRACKING
```

---

## KEY METRICS EXPLAINED

| Metric | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| **Gravity** | How many affiliates made sales in last 90 days. Higher = more competition but proven to convert |
| **EPC** | Earnings Per Click - average $ earned per 100 clicks |
| **APV** | Average Payout Value - average $ earned per sale |
| **Commission %** | Your cut of the sale price |
| **Cookie Duration** | How long after clicking your link credit is given (30-60 days typical) |
